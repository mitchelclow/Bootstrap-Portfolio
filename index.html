About Me

Mitchel Clow is a young professional in the Denver area with a passion for website design.

Having studied in Communications at Siena College, he finished his degree in 3 years in an eagerness to enter the workforce. At Siena, he learned trade skills in radio and television broadcast, along with AP style journalism and SEO.

He began culturing his coding skills when developing web pages for entertainment website Hypable.com. While working for Hypable, he was able to attend red carpet movie releases, and attend San Diego Comic-Con as press. He has also interviewed creators of Microsoft, Sony, and Nintendo video games.

Mitchel enrolled in a full stack development Coding Bootcamp at the University of Denver. Languages he learned there include Javascript jQuery, CSS, SASS, Grunt, PHP, RSS, HTML, Node, MySQL, Express, and React.

The 6 month intensive coding program gave him the opprotunity to push his technical skills to the limit. While challenging at times, he found that his expanding knowledge of code opened up a world of possibilities.
